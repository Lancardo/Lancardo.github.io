First author Optics Express, 2025

All-optical color image encryption using multimode fiber speckles and diffractive deep neural networks

Proposes an all-optical encryption and decryption framework that combines OAM encoding, multimode fiber speckle encryption, and dual D2NN decoding for robust, high-fidelity color image recovery.

  • Research focus: all-optical secure image transmission
  • Method keywords: OAM, MMF, D2NN, physical noise robustness
  • Role: sole first author

First author Optics and Lasers in Engineering, 2025

Color Image Encryption Using Spatial OAM Beam through a Multimode Fiber

Presents a color image encryption scheme using spatial degrees of freedom of OAM beams and multimode fiber propagation, with learning-based decryption and emphasis on resistance to unsupervised attack scenarios.

  • Research focus: optical color image encryption
  • Method keywords: spatial OAM, MMF, ADDL, anti-attack design
  • Role: sole first author

First author Physica Scripta, 2025

Data-driven prediction of dissipative soliton generation in Kerr fiber cavities

Uses DNN and LSTM models to accelerate prediction of pulse evolution in dispersion-managed Kerr fiber cavities, substantially reducing runtime while retaining high predictive accuracy.

  • Research focus: AI-accelerated nonlinear optical simulation
  • Method keywords: LLE, DNN, LSTM, soliton prediction
  • Role: sole first author
